My Story

My name is Ty Brown — Audio Visual Technician and Audio Engineer based in Tampa, FL. With over 10 years of professional experience in audio visual production, live sound engineering, and technical leadership, I've built a reputation for delivering polished results under pressure.

My career spans multiple domains: video directing and editing, live event AV production, music production, and technical team leadership. I've served as Audio Engineer and AV Lead at Arise Church in Brandon, FL, and as Audio Visual Director at AB Music, where I led both technical operations and marketing initiatives.

Whether it's FOH mixing for a 2,000-seat church service, producing a music video on a professional set, or setting up a crystal-clear sound system for an intimate wedding — I bring the same level of care and expertise to every job.

I'm also currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology at University of Massachusetts Global, continuously expanding my technical skillset at the intersection of AV and digital systems.
