My Story

The Guy Behind
the Board

I'm a freelance Audio Visual Technician and Audio Engineer based in Tampa, FL. Over the past 10+ years I've worked live events, run AV for churches, directed video on professional sets, and helped people put on everything from small backyard parties to mid-size productions.

I've served as Audio Engineer and AV Lead at Arise Church in Brandon, FL, and as Audio Visual Director at AB Music, handling technical operations and keeping things running night after night. I also currently work as a contract AV Technician for several local clients.

I started Ty Brown AV because I kept seeing people overpay big companies for simple event AV - or worse, try to make do with consumer gear that didn't deliver. There's a real gap between "rent some Bluetooth speakers" and "hire a full production company," and that's exactly where I operate.

If you need someone who knows what they're doing, shows up on time, communicates clearly, and takes your event seriously - I'd love to work with you.
