Meet the Founder

Ty Brown

Ty Brown is the founder and lead technician of Cornerstone AV Services. With over 10 years of professional experience in audio engineering, AV production, and technical operations, Ty brings a depth of knowledge that spans live events, broadcast, worship production, and residential systems.

His background includes serving as Audio Engineer and AV Lead at Arise Church in Brandon, FL, and Audio Visual Director at AB Music — roles that required managing complex technical environments, leading volunteer teams, and delivering consistent results under pressure.

Cornerstone AV Services was founded on the belief that quality audio visual support should be accessible, reliable, and tailored to each client — whether that's a growing church, a corporate event, or a homeowner looking to upgrade their space.
